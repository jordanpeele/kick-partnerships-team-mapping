## 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

- **Is KICK partnerships centralized or distributed?** Semi-centralized. Creator partnerships funnel through Andrew Santamaria (Head of Strategic Partnerships / KICK Studios), but major deals are CEO-driven (Ed Craven personally negotiates top-tier signings). Regional partnerships are being built out through hiring Partnerships Coordinators for LATAM, MENA, and other markets.

- **Where do creator deals likely sit?** Under Andrew Santamaria's team (KICK Studios / Strategic Partnerships), which reports directly to Ed Craven. For mega-deals ($10M+), Ed Craven is personally involved. Trainwreckstv provides grassroots creator recruitment and community advocacy.

- **How mature is the org?** Early-to-mid stage. Only 5 named leadership positions on TheOrg. Active hiring for Partnerships Coordinators, Influencer Marketing Managers, and Esports Sponsorships roles suggests the team is still being built. The shift from lump-sum mega-deals to a merit-based Partner Program (2025) signals organizational maturation.

- **Financial scale:** Easygo posted A$257M (~US$171M) net profit for FY2025 (ending June 30, 2025). Ed Craven stated on X that KICK Partner Program payouts are approaching **$150M** to thousands of creators (Source: [@StakeEddie](https://x.com/StakeEddie/status/1988753175667781921), NEXT.io). KICK has hit **26% global livestreaming market share** with over 1.1 billion watch hours in a quarter.

- **Key insight: who actually holds power in creator deals?** **Ed Craven** holds ultimate decision-making authority. **Andrew Santamaria** is the operational partnerships lead. **Trainwreckstv** has informal but significant influence on creator recruitment. For the biggest deals, talent agencies (especially **Evolved Talent Agency** -- Ryan Morrison & Sebastien Delvaux) negotiate directly with Craven.

## 6. RELATIONSHIP MAP (TEXT)

### How a Major Creator Signing Likely Works:

```
Creator/Agency reaches out
    ↓
kickpartners@kick.com (or direct contact with Santamaria)
    ↓
Andrew Santamaria evaluates (strategic fit, audience, metrics)
    ↓
For deals >$1M: Ed Craven gets involved directly
    ↓
Craven + Agency negotiate terms (Evolved Talent Agency is key counterparty)
    ↓
Nicolas Carbajal / Brais Pena Sanchez review commercial/financial terms
    ↓
Contract signed
    ↓
Rohan Gole / Darshil Kerai handle technical onboarding & integration
    ↓
Andrew Santamaria's team manages ongoing relationship
    ↓
Oliver Tierney handles esports-specific accounts
    ↓
Trainwreckstv provides community amplification & public endorsement
```

### How the Partner Program (KPP) Works:

```
Creator meets requirements (75 CCV, 30hrs streamed, 25 subs, 250 followers)
    ↓
Emails kickpartners@kick.com
    ↓
Manual review by partnerships team (1-2 weeks)
    ↓
If approved: 95/5 sub split + hourly pay based on algorithm
    ↓
For Fast Track: Apply via OTK/Cross Realm (100+ avg viewers, no hour requirements)
```

### Deal Evolution (2022-2026):
```
Phase 1 (2022-2023): Mega-deals → xQc $100M, Adin $30-100M+, Amouranth $30-40M
Phase 2 (2024): Regional expansion → LATAM/MENA signings (westcol, El Spreen)
Phase 3 (2025+): Merit-based → No more lump sums. KPP pays ~$16/hr base.
```
