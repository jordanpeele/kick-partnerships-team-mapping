# KICK (Kick.com) Team Research - Partnerships, Creator Relations, Marketing, Business Development & Growth

**Research Date:** March 19, 2026
**Methodology:** Extensive web search across LinkedIn, news articles, corporate databases (TheOrg, RocketReach, CBInsights, ZoomInfo), Wikipedia, and industry publications (Dexerto, Tubefilter, StreamsCharts).

---

## IMPORTANT CAVEATS

- **Only people actually found in search results are listed below.** No names have been invented or hallucinated.
- Sources are explicitly cited for each person. Where information is inferred vs. directly stated, this is noted.
- Some third-party databases (especially exa.ai/websets) appear to contain AI-generated or speculative content. Entries like "Jane Smith (CTO)" and "David Chen (CPO)" from exa.ai were excluded because they appear to be fabricated placeholder profiles -- no corroborating sources exist anywhere else.
- "Takashi Kousaka" appeared in some searches as "President" of Kick, but this refers to a DIFFERENT company called "Kick, Inc." (sold to Sony), not Kick streaming. He has been excluded.
- "Daniela Klein" appeared as "CEO of KICk" but this appears to be a different company (a PR/ESG consultancy), not the streaming platform. She has been excluded.
- "Conrad Wadowski" is CEO of a different company called "Kick" (an AI bookkeeping service). He has been excluded.
- "Harold Masinsky" appeared as CEO of "Kick" on RocketReach but appears to be associated with a different entity. Excluded.

## CONFIRMED KICK STREAMING TEAM MEMBERS

### 1. Ed Craven (Edward Craven)
- **Title:** CEO and Co-Founder
- **Sources:** [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ed_Craven), [TheOrg](https://theorg.com/org/kick-com-inc), [Kick Help Center](https://help.kick.com/en/articles/12273364-how-kick-com-started-origins-founders-streamer-success), [Sportskeeda](https://www.sportskeeda.com/esports/news-who-ed-craven-everything-need-know-kick-s-co-founder), [Medium](https://medium.com/@edcraven22)
- **Details:** Born 1995, Australian billionaire entrepreneur. Co-founded Kick in 2022 alongside Bijan Tehrani. Also co-founder of online casino Stake.com. Central to Kick's strategic trajectory and operational launch since December 2022. Led the design and execution of Kick's product roadmap, secured high-visibility streamer partnership deals. Holds 33.33% ownership of Kick through Ashwood Holdings.
- **Location:** Melbourne, Australia
- **Previous roles:** Co-founded Primedice (2013, crypto dice game), co-founded Easygo (2016, online casino games), co-founded Stake (2017).
- **Net worth:** A$4.51 billion (Feb 2025, AFR).
- **Relevance to partnerships:** As CEO, oversees all major partnership decisions. Has personally been involved in high-profile deals (MrBeast, UFC, Sauber F1, Everton FC).

### 2. Bijan Tehrani
- **Title:** Co-Founder (exact executive title less publicly defined than Craven's)
- **Sources:** [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bijan_Tehrani_(entrepreneur)), [Kick Help Center](https://help.kick.com/en/articles/12273364-how-kick-com-started-origins-founders-streamer-success), [GrowthNavigate](https://www.growthnavigate.com/who-owns-kick), [AffCatalog](https://affcatalog.com/en/person/bijan-tehrani/)
- **Details:** Co-founded Kick in 2022 with Ed Craven. Controls 66.67% of Kick through his direct stake in Easygo Entertainment. Born in the US to Iranian parents, later moved to Australia. Described as "a driving force behind Kick's business model and streamer-first revenue distribution, bringing in vital early partnerships and funding."
- **Location:** Melbourne, Australia
- **Previous roles:** Co-founded Primedice, co-founded Easygo, co-founded Stake.com. Previously a principal investor at TechBridge Ventures (per exa.ai, treat with some caution).
- **Net worth:** $2.8 billion (Forbes, May 2025).
- **Relevance to partnerships:** Major shareholder and strategic decision-maker for the platform.

### 3. Tyler Faraz Niknam (Trainwreckstv / Trainwreck)
- **Title:** Co-Founder and Head of Community (per exa.ai); Advisor (per earlier reporting)
- **Sources:** [Wikipedia (Trainwreckstv)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Trainwreckstv), [Esports.gg](https://esports.gg/news/streamers/trainwreck-announces-kick-a-new-streaming-platform-to-rival-twitch/), [Dexerto](https://www.dexerto.com/entertainment/trainwrecks-moves-kick-new-twitch-rival-2003504/), [Win.gg](https://win.gg/trainwreckstv-ownership-founding-kick/), [GameRant](https://gamerant.com/trainwreck-kick-creator-program/)

His role has evolved -- he updated his bio to call himself a "Kick co-founder" and is described as a partial owner. Heavily involved in promoting Kick and establishing the 95-5 revenue split. Devised community guidelines, loyalty initiatives, and direct marketing efforts. Served as the public face/spokesperson for Kick's Creator Program. Per exa.ai (treat with caution), led to a 150% surge in daily active users.
- **Location:** United States
- **Relevance to partnerships:** Key in creator acquisition and community engagement. Public-facing spokesperson for creator programs. Instrumental in shaping Kick's identity and attracting streamers.
